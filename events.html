Upcoming Events

The administration is pleased to inform all students that on friday june 25, 2022 will be the lunch of all political activities for the academy year, and as such all individuals of the senior class who are wishing to contest for the student council leadership are ask to apply with the program committee beginning now and june 24, 2022. Please note that any party that doesnot meet all criteriun set by the committee will not participate in the elections. signed: program chair fica.

Breaking News
Students Action Party SAP Denied
The students Action Party of FICA has been denied contesting for the 2022 2023 Students councis elections, In a press statement issued on thursday june 24, 2022 the chair of the program committee of FICA in person of Mr. Edward H. M. Kollie said that "The SAP presidential asparinat did not meet the minimum average for a contestent as set by the institution" therefore the party will not take part in this years students elections.
