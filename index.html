Who we are

We are Foundation In Christ Academy FICA a Christian School that aims to model the minds of the joung generations of this greatnation through education, The Institution has Day care, Elementary, Junior high, and Senior high. We also have some vocational courses that are being offered along with the regular academic calendar.    

Why we exist

At FICA we exist to prepare the future generations of this nation in surch a way that all students will be treated equal in their various desciplines and have the right to primary quality education for a better Liberia.

   
We offers several courses with our extral academic curriculum which include:

Comupter - We offer introduction to computer to the elementary division ranging from 3rd to 6th grade

MS Word - Introduction to MS word is being offered to the junior high division   

   
We at FICA we believe in Christ and as such we teach children how to live a Godly life and have respect for elderly so modesy is one of the pillars upon which we exist.  Our membrame verse is "Train up a child in the way he should go that when he is old he will not depart from it".
